Text Preprocessing:
Requires cleaning text, stemming or lemmatizing and converting text to numerical representation (text embedding).

Cleaning text:
Remove stop words, special characters and punctuation.

Stemming or Lemmatizing (Stemming faster, Lemmatizing more accurate):
Snowball stemmer, Lancaster stemmer (more aggressive), WordNet lemmatizer, SPaCy lemmatizer (handles large documents efficiently)

Text Embedding:
Bag of Words model (Count vectorisation or TF-IDF), Word embeddings (Word2Vec), Document embeddings (Doc2Vec), Pre-trained models (BERT, GPT), LDA
